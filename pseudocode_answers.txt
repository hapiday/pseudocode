Why do programmers use pseudocode?

Programmers use pseudocode because it helps to develop algorithms. They can brainstorm and break each step into simple, logical steps to outline the tasks before writing the actual code.

If you run pseudocode on your computer what would happen?

Pseudocode is meant for humans to read rather than machines, so it won't run on the computer.
